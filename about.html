About Me

Andrew Coutinho here — founder of ASF Visuals LLC, a certified drone and landscape photography studio blending artistry, precision, and storytelling.

With clients like the University of Pittsburgh, I create immersive visuals that highlight everything from city skylines to quiet natural scenes. My work is showcased through a fully custom-coded website I built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I recently completed a dual degree in Economics and Africana Studies at Pitt. My academic path took me across the world — including Tanzania as a Fulbright-Hays Scholar — where I studied African development, language, and culture hands-on.

On campus, I founded the Bodybuilding Club, organizing events, building partnerships, and growing a community of athletes. These leadership and project management skills translate directly into how I run ASF Visuals — with clarity, intention, and hustle.

I’m now expanding into fine art prints and brand visuals, while continuing to explore creative ways to connect people to place. Whether you're looking for standout visuals or a collaborative partner, I’d love to hear from you.
