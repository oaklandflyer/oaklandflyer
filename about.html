About Me

Hello! I’m Andrew Coutinho, the creative mind behind OaklandFlyer.

I specialize in capturing breathtaking moments through drone and landscape photography, blending storytelling with technology to reveal perspectives many never see. From towering skylines to quiet natural stillness, every shot is intentional and deeply personal.

I’m currently double-majoring in Economics and Africana Studies at the University of Pittsburgh, where I also founded the Bodybuilding Club. These experiences taught me how to build community, lead with purpose, and stay disciplined in my creative pursuits.

Outside of photography, I’m passionate about music, fitness, and innovation. Whether I’m flying a drone, composing visuals, or just exploring new landscapes, I’m driven by a love for the world’s textures, rhythms, and unexpected beauty.

If my work resonates with you, let’s connect. I’m always open to collaborations, bookings, and creative partnerships.
