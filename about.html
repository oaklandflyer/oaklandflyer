About Me

Hello! My name is Andrew Coutinho, and I’m the creative mind behind OaklandFlyer. I specialize in capturing breathtaking moments through drone and landscape photography, combining my passion for technology and storytelling to present the world from unique perspectives. Every image I create reflects my love for nature, architecture, and the artistry found in everyday scenes. I’m currently pursuing a double major in Economics and Africana Studies at the University of Pittsburgh. Alongside my academic pursuits, I’ve honed my leadership skills as the founder and president of the University’s Bodybuilding Club, where I’ve built a thriving community around health, discipline, and personal growth. Photography is more than just a hobby for me—it’s a way to connect with others and share the beauty I see in the world. Whether it’s the golden hues of a Pittsburgh sunset or the intricate details of cityscapes captured from above, my work is driven by a deep curiosity and appreciation for the visual world. Beyond the lens, I’m passionate about music and drone technology, and I enjoy merging creativity with innovation in all aspects of my life. My goal is to inspire others through my work and to collaborate with individuals and brands that share my vision for creating meaningful and impactful visual stories. Thank you for visiting my website!
